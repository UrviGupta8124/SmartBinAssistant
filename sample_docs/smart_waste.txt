EcoTech Smart Dustbin - Smart Waste Management Project

=== PROBLEM STATEMENT ===
Delhi faces interconnected issues like pollution, health risks, flooding, and land degradation, all linked to poor waste handling.
The city generates 11,000+ tons of waste daily, but segregation at source is minimal.
Major landfills (Ghazipur, Bhalswa, Okhla) are overloaded and unsafe.
Open dumping, burning, and plastic waste clog drains, causing air pollution and urban floods.
Methane emissions and land loss from landfills degrade the environment and quality of life.

=== PROPOSED SOLUTION ===
IoT Smart Bin for automated segregation and Eco-Friendly disposal.
CNN-based AI for real-time waste classification.
Mobile App for tracking, rewards & pickup and monitor bin status.

Challenges and How We Address Them:
- High Manufacturing Costs: Bulk procurement, local sourcing, and optimized hardware design to lower expenses.
- User Adoption & Awareness: Awareness drives, CSR sponsorships, and reward partnerships to encourage usage.
- Maintenance & Reliability: Waterproof/protected sensors, modular parts for easy replacement, and annual servicing plans.
- Deployment & Logistics: Partner with municipalities & logistics providers, design easy plug-and-play installation.

=== TECHNICAL APPROACH ===

Hardware & Data Collection:
- Waste Detection & Classification: Camera and IR sensors identify waste using a TensorFlow CNN model (~90% accuracy).
- Real-Time Segregation: Automatic classification into Organic, Recyclable, and Hazardous waste categories.
- Mechanical Sorting System: Servo motors enable precise routing into a three-compartment bin with automatic lid control.
- Hygiene & Safety Design: Anti-contamination sealing mechanism prevents mixing and odor leakage.
- System Control & Communication: ESP32 microcontroller with WiFi, using MQTT protocol for real-time data transmission to the cloud.

Hardware Components:
- ESP32 CAM (with camera module)
- Raspberry Pi
- IR Sensor
- White LED
- Buzzer
- Servo 1 and Servo 2

Frontend & User Experience:
- Citizen App (EcoScan): Built using React Native for Android and iOS.
- EcoIO: Municipal dashboard web application with real-time map, analytics, and route optimization data.
- Seamless Integration: Data flows from hardware to backend to user screen in real-time.

EcoTech Smart Dustbin addresses this challenge by enabling AI-based segregation at source, real-time monitoring, and data-driven municipal operations.
